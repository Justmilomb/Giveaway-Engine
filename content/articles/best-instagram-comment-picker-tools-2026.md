## What to Look For in an Instagram Comment Picker

Before we dive into the tools, here's what actually matters:

### 1. **True Randomness (Not Pseudo-Random)**
Most tools use `Math.random()`, which is predictable and can be manipulated. Look for tools using **cryptographic randomness** (Web Crypto API, hardware entropy sources).

### 2. **Fraud Detection**
Fake accounts, duplicate entries, and spam comments can ruin your giveaway. The best tools automatically flag suspicious entries.

### 3. **Transparent Pricing**
Some tools charge per giveaway. Others have hidden fees. Be wary of "free" tools that sell your data or require sharing their posts.

### 4. **Instagram API Compliance**
Instagram's API has strict rules. Tools that violate these risk getting your account banned. Always choose tools that follow Instagram's Terms of Service.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Using Free Tools That Sell Your Data
If it's free, you're the product. Some "free" tools collect your followers' emails and sell them to marketers.

### ❌ Picking Winners Manually
Manually selecting winners is biased (even if you don't mean to be). You'll subconsciously favor names you recognize or comments you liked.

### ❌ Using Tools That Violate Instagram's TOS
Instagram's API has strict rules. Tools that scrape comments without proper authorization can get your account banned.

### ❌ Not Verifying Winners
Always check that the winner:
- Follows your account (if required)
- Tagged friends (if required)
